Storytelling 101

Instant Feedback

Resume, Interview & Negotiation Services

Resume Review

Everything starts with the experience and knowledge that you bring to the table. The resume review informs the advice we provide, and we help you craft the resume so that it stands out.

Skills Assessment

The Product Manager role requires a diverse set of skills. It is sometimes required that you acquire another skillset (or more) to increase your chances of success.

Application Strategy

Standout from the competition. We'll discuss key ways to set-up your job search to maximize impact so that you can more effectively target companies and secure an interview.

Mock Behavioral Interviews

We'll help you identify the most impactful stories to share. Every company not named Google asks Behavioral questions during the interview. Practice your responses and learn to better craft your story. Each mock interview is run the same way you'll experience it live.

Mock Product Interviews

Learn to put it all together with proven tips and frameworks. The distinguishing part of the product manager interview is the Product / case questions which require a cross-section of areas such as business, analytics, engineering, and design. Each mock interview is run the same way you'll experience it live.

Offer Negotiation

Hit your target. When you receive an offer, learn what is or is not expected so that you can feel good when you negotiate and accept the offer. Or if it makes more sense to decline the offer, we'll help you strategize that conversation.
